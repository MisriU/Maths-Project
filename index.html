Q U A D R I L A T E R A L S



SQUARES

Properties of a Square:

- All sides are the same

- All angles equal 90 degrees

- All sides lengths are the same

- All exterior angles are 90 degrees



RECTANGLES

Properties of a Rectangle:

- 2 parallel sets of sides

- All angles equal 90 degrees

- 2 pairs of equal side lengths

- All exterior angles are 90 degrees



PARALLELOGRAMS

Properties of a Parallelogram:

- 2 parallel sets of sides

- Opposite sides are congruent

- 2 pairs of equal side lengths

- Opposite angels are congruent



RHOMBUS

Properties of a Rhombus:

- 2 parallel sets of sides

- Opposite sides are congruent

- All four sides are the same length

- Opposite angels are congruent

- Opposite interior angles are the same

TRAPEZIUM

Properties of a Trapezium:

- The bases are always parallel to each other

- Both the diagonals always intersect each other

- The sum of adjacent interior angles of a trapezium is 180°

- The interior angles in a trapezium always sum up to 360°



KITE

Properties of a Kite:

- 2 diagonals that intersect each other at right angles

- Angles opposite to the main diagonal are equal

- No parallel sides

- Can be viewed as a pair of congruent triangles with a common base
